Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find volunteer opportunities that match my interests? +
Use our filter system to browse by cause area, location, and time commitment. Each opportunity listing includes detailed information about the organization and what to expect.
How does hour tracking work? +
After volunteering, simply log your hours through our tracking form. Your contribution counts toward Colorado's 150,000-hour goal and shows the economic impact of service.
What is the Colorado Day of Service? +
Our signature annual event in October brings together thousands of volunteers across Colorado for a coordinated day of service. It's a celebration of community and leads into Colorado Gives Day.
Can my company participate as a group? +
Absolutely! Many of our partner organizations welcome corporate volunteer groups. Look for opportunities marked with "Groups Welcome" or contact us for custom corporate volunteering experiences.
How do nonprofits become partners? +
We partner with organizations funded by ABCDEF foundations that have established volunteer programs. Interested organizations should contact us to learn about partnership requirements.
What is the 150,000-hour goal? +
To celebrate Colorado's 150th anniversary in 2026, we're aiming for 150,000 volunteer hours across the state‚Äîrepresenting $5.8M in community impact. Every hour you contribute brings us closer to this historic milestone!

Make Your Impact Count

We want to recognize the powerful ways you are giving back. Our goal is 150,000 volunteer hours representing $5.8M donated to charitable causes. Your input helps us hit (and hopefully exceed!) these goals to show the momentum of our community.
